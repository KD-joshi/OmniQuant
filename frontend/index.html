Understanding the Greeks:

  • Delta (Δ): How much the option price changes for a $1 move in the underlying stock (e.g., 0.50 means it moves like owning 50 shares).
  • Gamma (Γ): How fast Delta accelerates. This is highest when the stock is close to your Strike Price.
  • Theta (Θ): Time decay. Exactly how much money this option loses every single day just by sitting in your account.
  • Vega (ν): Sensitivity to fear. How much the option price instantly spikes if overall market volatility increases by 1%.
