Methodology · 04

About OmniQuant

Built for precision. OmniQuant unifies classical financial mathematics with modern quantitative technical analysis to help you make informed decisions.

01

Monte Carlo Simulations

We generate thousands of random walks using Geometric Brownian Motion based on a stock's historical drift and volatility. This allows us to accurately compute the Value at Risk (VaR), helping you understand your maximum expected downside in normal market conditions.

02

Black-Scholes Pricing

Our options pricer implements the classical Black-Scholes partial differential equation to determine the theoretical fair value of European contracts. We also compute first and second-order derivatives (The Greeks) to measure sensitivity to price, time decay, and implied volatility.

03

Quantitative Screening

The screener fetches live market data to compute technical indicators like the Relative Strength Index (RSI), Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD), and 50-day Simple Moving Averages. These metrics are fed into our proprietary algorithmic intelligence engine to recommend actionable strategies.
